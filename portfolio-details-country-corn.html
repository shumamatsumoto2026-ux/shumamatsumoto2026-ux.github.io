Country Corn

Project Overview

Developed a robust, high-performance digital presence for Country Corn Australia, a leading frozen produce supplier. The goal was to create a unified brand hub that bridges the gap between their wholesale operations (Harvestime) and their direct-to-consumer retail arm (Frozberries).

  • Client: Country Corn
  • Website: https://countrycorn.com.au/
The Challenge

The client needed a modern, mobile-responsive interface that could effectively communicate their 15+ years of industry excellence while providing clear "call-to-action" paths for two distinct user bases: home shoppers and commercial businesses.

Technical Stack & Features
  • Core Engine: WordPress CMS (v6.9.1) for flexible content management. Mobile optimise.
  • Architecture: Custom-developed theme utilizing WPBakery Page Builder for bespoke grid layouts and section designs.
  • Performance & UI:
    • SVG Integration:Implementation of lightweight vector graphics for contact and social icons to ensure crisp rendering on retina displays.
    • Typography: Integration of Google Fonts (Poppins & Pacifico) to balance corporate professionalism with a friendly, "farm-fresh" aesthetic.
    • Responsive Design: Mobile-first CSS architecture with custom breakpoints for seamless browsing across all devices.
  • SEO & Data: * Comprehensive Schema.org (JSON-LD) integration to enhance local search visibility and "Organization" rich snippets. Open Graph (OG) meta-tagging for optimized social media sharing.
