about myselt

I am a Senior UX Engineer with over 15 years of experience at Mastercard, dedicated to bridging the gap between high-fidelity design and scalable frontend code. My career has evolved from digital design into building enterprise-level design systems (the 'Made' framework) and leading Design Operations to accelerate product delivery.

I recently completed the Google UX Design Professional Certificate to deepen my research and accessibility expertise. I believe that a perfect user experience isn't just about how it looks in Figma, but how it performs in the hands of the user. I thrive on solving complex technical challenges while ensuring the 'human' element of the interface is never lost.

  • Frontend Engineering:

    Specialist in React, Native (Mobile), JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, Sass/SCSS, wordpress and PHP.

  • UX/UI Design:

    User research, wireframing, high-fidelity prototyping, and Experience KPI tracking. (Google UX Design Professional Certificate).

  • Design Systems:

    Expert in building and scaling enterprise UI libraries (Made) using Design Tokens, React, and Storybook.

  • Design Operations & Strategy:

    Intake & resourcing optimization, project lifecycle management (Studio/Foundry stages), speed-to-market acceleration, and cross-functional handover documentation.
